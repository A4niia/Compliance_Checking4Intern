📝 Text Simplification

Purpose: Remove OCR artifacts and normalize text extracted from PDF documents. Poor text quality from OCR leads to LLM classification failures. This preprocessing step removes line breaks mid-sentence, normalizes whitespace, and cleans formatting issues.

Impact: Improved classification accuracy by 15 percentage points (pp). 88% of rules (85/97) were affected by text simplification.
